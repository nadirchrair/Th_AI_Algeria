About The Conference

The 1st Edition of The Biggest AI Conference in Algeria

AI in Algeria is a 3-day premier event that will bring together the brightest minds and international experts in Artificial Intelligence from Algeria and around the world. The conference will feature a mix of keynotes by renowned speakers, panel discussions, workshops, and networking opportunities. With a focus on AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) as well as AI in Universities, this event offers a platform to explore the latest advancements, applications, and integration of AI technologies. Attendees will gain insights into cutting-edge research, best practices, and strategies for leveraging AI, fostering AI education, and driving innovation in this rapidly evolving landscape. The event will be held at the Méridien Oran Hotel & Convention Centre in Oran, Algeria.

  • AI in Algeria, Strategy & Vision for 2035
  • AI & Large Language Models
  • AI in The Algerian University
  • Location Le Méridien Convention Centre, Oran, Algeria
  • Date & Time 9am - 4pm, 14th - 16th Oct, 2024
  • Speakers 12 National & International Professionals
